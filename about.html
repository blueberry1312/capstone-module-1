VIDEOGAMES GLOBAL SUMMIT 2023

At present, there are various issues that concern professionals and students, as well as various video game companies around the world, such as: where should business strategies be directed for the development of comprehensive entertainment systems and, thus, be able to monetize the interest of the traditional public and even of new consumer segments.
